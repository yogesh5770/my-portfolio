# Deploying Your Portfolio For Free 🚀

Your portfolio is fully built with industry-standard code. To make it live on the internet so you can send the link to shops and salons, follow these simple steps:

## Option 1: Netlify (Simplest - Highly Recommended)
1. Go to [Netlify.com](https://www.netlify.com/) and create a free account.
2. Once logged in, go to the **Sites** tab.
3. Scroll down to the area that says **"Want to deploy a new site without connecting to Git? Drag and drop your site folder here."**
4. Open your File Explorer, go to `C:\`, and drag the `my portfolio` folder directly into that box on the website.
5. **Boom!** Your site is live. You can even change the URL name in "Site Settings".

## Option 2: GitHub Pages (Best for Developers)
1. Create a free account on [GitHub.com](https://github.com/).
2. Create a new "Repository" named `portfolio`.
3. Upload your `index.html`, `style.css`, and `script.js` files to that repository.
4. Go to **Settings > Pages**.
5. Select the **main branch** as the source and click "Save".
6. In a few minutes, your site will be live at `yourusername.github.io/portfolio`.
